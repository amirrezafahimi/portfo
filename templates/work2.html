002 : Blue Book for Bulldozers

This challenge was posted in 2013, you can check it out in kaggle's own page. The goal of the contest was to predict the sale price of a particular piece of heavy equipment at auction based on it's usage, equipment type, and configuration. T he data was sourced from auction result postings and includes information on usage and equipment configurations. you can check the project on my github page
